About Me

Hi! I'm Christine.

I live in Portland, OR with my husband. Both of us are a transplants to Oregon, but I have been here for 10 years; I was born in California, and grew up in Woodinville, Washington.

I am currently a student at Epicodus, a programming school downtown. Before that, I was a Design and Development Engineer (aka Process Engineer) at a medical manufacturing company in Lake Oswego. I found my passion for programming there while implementing machine control software to the manufacturing floor. I decided to invest in myself and grow my skills in programming before taking the big leap - shifting my career to software development.

I am passionate about learning, and thrive in a dynamic environment that requires critical thinking and problem solving.
